#+title: Kronos effect
#+date: [2024-01-31 Wed 04:17]
#+filetags: concept
Term coined by [[id:5a68c870-82d2-426e-b6f6-1d4150aa6d7b][Tim Wu]] in [[id:807632b3-5424-4d5c-8cea-fa3665b377b1][The Master Switch]].
Companies that establish early dominance in with disruptive innovation will do everything in their power to maintain the first-mover advantage, often by /eating/ their children and stopping new innovation to disrupt their business.
